Optimizing Supply Chain Software

Client: Elemica

Overview

I was brought onto the design team at Elemica to build out a component library and contribute as a product designer on new web applications.

My Approach

I started by auditing all existing product design files and compiling initial atomic components. I continued to maintain the library as we added new features. Some of the web applications I worked on were a risk management software that forecasted global events impact on a shipping route, as well as a mobile application for truck drivers to check in at various stages of a shipment.
