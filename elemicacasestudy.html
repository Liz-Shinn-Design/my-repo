Redesigning a Supply Chain SaaS Web App

UX/UI

Enterprise Design

The Brief

I worked with Elemica to build a product component library and product features that empowered logistics teams to track and make decisions about shipments.

In my role as a UX/UI Designer, I was responsible for creating a design system that would allow the design team to quickly mock up new features and layouts. I also worked on several key projects, including a risk management dashboard and a shipment tracking app.

Challenges

  1. 1 Users could not easily navigate complex tables of data with no visual hierarchy to provide structure.
  2. 2 Product interfaces were not flexible. Data was cut off or hidden from users.
The solution

I built variable layouts that could respond to many types of data, and a central component library that the design team could use to quickly mock up designs.

The Impact

The component library was adopted by the team and used to create all new features. It helped to keep designs consistent and improved the overall user experience. The product team was also able to ship new features faster.

I built out user personas to consider user Jobs to Be Done in the design process.
