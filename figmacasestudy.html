My design approach

In my research, I found that YouTube tutorials, Figma documentation, and Figma Community files were geared towards professional designers. They were too complex and assumed a lot of existing knowledge.

I streamlined the material to the essential workflows my colleagues needed. I designed and ran several remote workshops facilitated via Zoom. I received positive feedback from participants who said they felt less overwhelmed.

The workshop results

After seeing success internally, I wanted to provide the workshop to a larger audience who may experience this same problem at their organizations.

I published the file to Figma Community along with workshop documentation; it's been downloaded 700+ times and viewed 2,000+ times!
