About Me

I have 9+ years of experience designing effective digital products across the web3, supply chain, healthcare, and fintech industries. In my past year of work, I focused on the spot where web3 and healthcare intersect — a privacy-forward, menstrual tracking app called Embody.

I'm based in Atlanta, GA and working remotely has allowed me to work and travel around the world. I enjoy connecting people, and I founded the organization Ladies Work Remote to bring women who work from home together for weekly coworking events.
