Designing a Branded Infographics System

Client Mezo Network

Timeframe 2024

Services
路 Design Systems
路 Infographics Design
路 Design Mentorship
路 Documentation

Summary
I worked closely with the Mezo marketing and product teams to develop a branded visuals system they could use to meet their content strategy goals of transparency and user friendliness.

My Approach

My design goals for the project were to build a reusable, easy-to-understand template system in Figma that the marketing team could use themselves, and a design resource would initially be needed for review only. This would prevent a bottleneck and allow both the design team and the marketing team to meet their goals. I referenced Pinterest, infographics books, and the client brand deck to ensure the charts would align well with the Mezo brand.

The visuals would be used both on the Mezo blog and in product documentation on GitBook. I worked closely with a member of the marketing team so I could uncover where the pain points were so we could systematize design work.

The Results

Initially, we frequently checked in to get feedback and improve the system. As I built more infographic templates and incorporated more varieties of data sets, it continued to expand in flexibility.
